What We Do

Roadside Services You Can Count On

From a dead battery to a blown tire, Monkey Wrench has the equipment and expertise to get you back on the road quickly and safely.

Towing

Need a tow? We provide safe, damage-free towing for cars, trucks, and SUVs. We transport your vehicle to the shop or location of your choice.

Flat Tire Service

Blown a tire? We'll swap on your spare quickly and safely so you can get back on the road. No spare? We'll tow you to the nearest tire shop.

Jump Start / Battery Service

Dead battery? We carry professional jump-start equipment and can have your engine running again in minutes, day or night.

Fuel Delivery

Ran out of gas? We'll bring enough fuel to get you to the nearest gas station. No need to walk or wait for a ride — we come to you.

Lockout Service

Locked your keys inside? We use professional tools to unlock your vehicle without damage. We'll have you back inside fast.

Winching & Recovery

Off the road or stuck in mud? Our recovery equipment can pull your vehicle out safely so you don't need a full tow.

Don't see your situation listed? Give us a call — we handle all kinds of roadside emergencies.
